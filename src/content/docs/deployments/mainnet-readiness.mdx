# Mainnet Readiness Roadmap

⚠️ **IMPORTANT: SSID mainnet deployment is NOT LIVE.** This page describes the **planned** production readiness gates and timeline. No SSID services are currently operating on Ethereum mainnet or Polygon mainnet. All current testing is on **Sepolia and Mumbai testnets only.**

## Current Status

| Phase | Timeline | Status | Notes |
|-------|----------|--------|-------|
| **Phase 0: Planning** | Q1 2026 | ✅ Complete | Roadmap defined, gate structure finalized |
| **Phase 1: Testnet** | Q2 2026 | 🟡 In Progress | Sepolia/Mumbai deployments, integration testing |
| **Phase 2: Audit & Security** | Q3 2026 | ❌ Not Started | Third-party smart contract audit (3-4 weeks) |
| **Phase 3: Mainnet Prep** | Q4 2026 | ❌ Not Started | Mainnet contract deployments (non-production) |
| **Phase 4: Soft Launch** | Q1 2027 | ❌ Not Started | Limited mainnet rollout, community validator set |
| **Phase 5: Full Launch** | TBD | ❌ Not Started | General availability on mainnet |

**No go-live date is set. Mainnet launch requires passing all gates in Phase 2–4.**

## Mainnet Readiness Gates

### Gate 1: Testnet Stability (Q2 2026)

**Requirement:** All core flows stable on Sepolia + Mumbai for ≥2 weeks  
**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ DID registration and resolution &lt;100ms latency
- ✅ VC issuance cycle &lt;5s end-to-end
- ✅ Verification success rate ≥99.9%
- ✅ Zero critical bugs in integration test suite (18+ tests)
- ✅ Orchestrator uptime ≥99.95%
- ✅ All API endpoints pass contract tests

**Owner:** Platform Engineering  
**Approval:** Technical Steering Committee

### Gate 2: Security Audit (Q3 2026)

**Requirement:** Independent third-party smart contract audit  
**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ Audit firm selected (reputable: OpenZeppelin, Trail of Bits, CertiK, or equivalent)
- ✅ Audit scope: 100% of contracts in 03_core and 20_foundation
- ✅ No critical/high-severity issues remaining
- ✅ Audit report published (redacted version in docs)
- ✅ All recommendations addressed or documented with risk acceptance

**Estimated Cost:** $25K–$75K USD  
**Estimated Duration:** 3–4 weeks  
**Owner:** Security & Compliance  
**Approval:** Board + Legal

### Gate 3: Regulatory Compliance (Q3–Q4 2026)

**Requirement:** Compliance with eIDAS, MiCA, GDPR, and jurisdiction-specific rules  
**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ eIDAS Annex I alignment documented (qualified trust services)
- ✅ MiCA classification (token = transferable; governance = utility)
- ✅ GDPR data processing agreement (DPA) finalized
- ✅ Identity verification flow meets KYC thresholds
- ✅ AML/CFT screening integrated (Sumsub, iDenfy, or equivalent)
- ✅ Privacy impact assessment (PIA) completed

**Owner:** Legal & Compliance  
**Approval:** External Counsel + Regulatory Advisor

### Gate 4: Operational Readiness (Q4 2026)

**Requirement:** Production infrastructure and runbooks in place  
**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ Kubernetes cluster configured for mainnet (HA, auto-scaling, monitoring)
- ✅ Vault instance hardened (HSM, backup, disaster recovery)
- ✅ Observability stack live (Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry, alerts)
- ✅ Incident response runbooks documented and tested
- ✅ On-call rotation established (24/7 coverage)
- ✅ Backup and recovery procedures tested monthly

**Owner:** DevOps & Infrastructure  
**Approval:** VP Operations

### Gate 5: Go-Live Approval (Q1 2027)

**Requirement:** Executive and stakeholder sign-off  
**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ All prior gates (1–4) passed
- ✅ Risk register reviewed and accepted
- ✅ Insurance/indemnity arrangements finalized
- ✅ Launch communications and support plan approved
- ✅ Soft-launch success (validators, early partners)
- ✅ Board/DAO vote for full launch (if applicable)

**Owner:** Executive Leadership  
**Approval:** CEO, Board, Community (DAO vote)

## Pre-Launch Checklist

### Infrastructure (2 weeks before launch)

- [ ] All services deployed to mainnet (non-production)
- [ ] Contracts verified on Etherscan / PolygonScan
- [ ] Monitoring dashboards live and green
- [ ] Load test: 1000 concurrent users, pass &lt;1% error rate
- [ ] Backup restore test: RTO &lt;1 hour, RPO &lt;15 min
- [ ] SSL certificates valid and auto-renewal configured

### Security (1 week before launch)

- [ ] Penetration test completed
- [ ] OWASP Top 10 scan: zero critical/high findings
- [ ] Secrets rotation: all API keys, DB passwords, private keys regenerated
- [ ] Audit logs enabled and monitored
- [ ] Rate limiting configured (API 1000 req/sec per user)
- [ ] DDoS mitigation in place (Cloudflare, AWS Shield, or equivalent)

### Legal & Compliance (2 weeks before launch)

- [ ] Terms of Service finalized
- [ ] Privacy Policy approved
- [ ] Smart contract license (MIT/Apache/GPL)
- [ ] Liability waiver (if required by jurisdiction)
- [ ] Support SLA documented

### Communications (3 weeks before launch)

- [ ] Launch announcement drafted
- [ ] Mainnet documentation complete (you are here)
- [ ] Help desk trained on common issues
- [ ] Social media coordination (Discord, Twitter, etc.)
- [ ] Community office hours scheduled

## Risk Register (High-Level)

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Smart contract exploit | Critical | Third-party audit, formal verification (optional) |
| Regulatory action | High | Compliance gate (Gate 3), legal review |
| Network downtime | High | HA infrastructure, automatic failover, SLA monitoring |
| Liquidity shortage | Medium | DAO governance, token distribution plan |
| User loss of keys | Medium | Education, non-custodial design, recovery options |
| Reputational damage | High | Transparent roadmap, security-first culture, incident response |

## Mainnet Support Model

### Initial Launch Phase (Month 1–3)

- **Support Hours:** 24/7 on-call for critical issues
- **Response Time:** &lt;15 min for P0 (system down), &lt;1h for P1 (significant degradation)
- **Status Page:** Real-time updates on https://status.ssid.dev (example)
- **Escalation:** Dedicated Slack channel for community critical issues

### Steady State (Month 4+)

- **Support Hours:** Business hours (9-17 UTC) for general inquiries; 24/7 for security incidents
- **Response Time:** &lt;1h for critical, &lt;24h for non-critical
- **Community:** Self-service documentation, Discord/forum support

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When is mainnet launching?**  
A: No launch date is set. All phases must complete successfully. Target range is Q1–Q2 2027, but dates may slip if gates are not passed.

**Q: Can I use SSID on mainnet now?**  
A: No. SSID is testnet-only (Sepolia/Mumbai). Mainnet deployment is under development.

**Q: How much will mainnet cost?**  
A: Gas fees depend on network conditions. Initial estimates: $0.10–$1.00 USD per operation (DID registration, VC issuance).

**Q: Will my testnet DIDs work on mainnet?**  
A: No. Testnet and mainnet are separate. You will need to re-register on mainnet after launch.

**Q: What if a gate fails?**  
A: The timeline slips. We will not launch without passing all gates, even if it means delaying months.
