# Token Distribution & Fairness

This page documents the fair-distribution rule: how SSID allocates rewards based on verified impact rather than capital concentration.

## POFI (Proof of Fairness Indicator)

POFI determines priority for discretionary reward pools.

**Formula:** `POFI = Historical Activity Level / Lifetime Rewards Accumulated`

| POFI value | Meaning | Effect |
|------------|---------|--------|
| **High** | Much activity, low rewards received | Higher priority for airdrops and bonuses |
| **Low** | High rewards already accumulated | Lower priority |

**Applies to:** community bonus pools, growth incentives, education grants.

**Does NOT affect:** governance voting rights or validator reward payouts.

## Impact-token binding

Rewards scale with verified impact, not capital.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Bound metrics** | Education completion, energy savings, CO2 reduction |
| **Concentration prevention** | Rewards scale with verified impact, not capital |
| **Configurability** | Governance votes can add, modify, or retire metrics |
| **Verification** | Outcomes attested via verifiable credentials |
